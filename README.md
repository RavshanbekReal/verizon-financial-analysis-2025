# Verizon Financial Analysis-2025
Comprehensive financial analysis of Verizon Communications Inc. using Excel. Includes liquidity, solvency, efficiency, profitability, and market ratios, plus cost of equity (CAPM &amp; DDM), cost of debt, and WACC. Compared with AT&amp;T Inc. and T-Mobile US to assess Verizon’s industry position and trends
This report evaluates the financial health and cost of capital of Verizon Communications Inc. using data from its Form 10-K and other reliable sources. Key financial ratios are calculated over the past three years to assess liquidity, solvency, efficiency, profitability, and market performance, and are compared with competitors AT&T Inc. and T-Mobile US, Inc. to determine Verizon’s relative position in the telecommunications industry. Based on this analysis, the report provides an overall assessment of the company’s financial condition, highlighting major trends, strengths, and weaknesses. In addition, the cost of equity is estimated using both CAPM and the Dividend Discount Model (DDM), while the cost of debt is derived from bond yield data; these are then combined to calculate the firm’s weighted average cost of capital (WACC). All calculations are performed in Excel and are clearly organized to support the findings and conclusions.
